Our Services

One on One Mentorship

Connect one-on-one with the student mentors from your dream College to make an informed decision while choosing your next step.

SOP and Resume Review

Your SOP and Resume speak for you. A well-curated resume increases your probability of getting selected. We help you curate your SOP and Resume through the bright minds currently studying in your desired college as per the college standards.

Live Webinars

We host regular live webinars on the roadmap to an MBA college. The mentors from different colleges share their MBA journey and the coveted hacks of cracking an interview. The speakers also facilitate live Q&A sessions addressing all the queries of the aspirants.

GD/WAT/PI Mock and Guidance

Our Mock PI and Guidance session with the current seniors from the college will help you break the ice before an interview giving a better perspective on tackling stressful interview questions.
